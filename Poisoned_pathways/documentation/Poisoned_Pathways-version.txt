Beta Version 5.2
- changes by morpheus562:
This is all of the script updates leaving original intent in-tact. The biggest divergeance is will-o-wisp where I have it use more spells based on difficulty level. I also updated the mummy to have an weapon assigned.
In addition to updating attack and spell targeting, I am also utilizing EEex for net new custom triggers. This includes:
•	Identifying if the current target is moving and therefore trying to kite the enemy so the enemy can focus on a creature that is not kiting.
•	Identifying if an player controlled character is casting a spell and attempting to direct physical attacks on that caster to force a spell interrupt.
•	New trigger to check if the best target to attack is the current target being attacked. If not, then the scripts will re-direct their attacks to the better target.
