Poisoned Pathways — Eldath’s Grotto Trade System (Summary)
==============================================================

What this feature does (in‑game)
--------------------------------
In Eldath’s Grotto, a dryad offers to retire “named” weapons so they can harm no one again.
When you hand a weapon to her, you receive Eldath’s blessing:

• Lesser (normal) weapon  →  +1 permanent maximum Hit Point (AC#PPH1).
• Legendary weapon        →  +2 permanent maximum Hit Points (AC#PPH2)
                             +1% permanent resistance to ALL physical damage types
                             (slashing, piercing, crushing, missile) (AC#PPGR).

No gold is paid. The weapon is removed from your inventory and “laid to rest”
Bonuses stack with each offering
