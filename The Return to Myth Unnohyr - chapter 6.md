# The Return to Myth Unnohyr

## Summary
After some absence of CHARNAME and their group, it is finally time to face Bodhi so they may return to Duskwood Dell for help. It is then we find out that our doings, or maybe Talona herself, woke something up at Myth Unnohyr—most likely a powerful wyrd, that took over the body of an old elven commander that fell in this unholy place. The High Priest is now visited by a High Ranger of Mielikki, who asks for assistance in fighting the creature. Will the pacifist temple of Eldath help the ranger, or is CHARNAME once again needed to make things right?

## Step by Step

1. **Chapter 6: Return to Duskwood Dell**  
   When CHARNAME visits Duskwood Dell in Chapter 6, they are informed about new disturbances in Myth Unnohyr. The Eldathyn priest expresses concern: while fighting the priest of Talona was regrettable, the violence must end. The High Ranger of Mielikki, however, remains adamant that something must be done.

2. **A Compromise**  
   A compromise is reached. The Eldathyn priest offers a unique sword—one that can slay the undead but cannot harm the living. The ranger, who had foreseen this escalation, has prepared a teleportation glyph attuned to Myth Unnohyr. He uses it to send the group inside the sealed zone where the wyrd now dwells. Due to the zone’s wild and dead magic, the ranger cannot assist further. The party must rely on what they bring—and what they find.

3. **Inside the Ruins**  
   Once within the sealed area, things are worse than expected. The wyrd has summoned the bloodied spirits of ancient elves. The group locates the wyrd and engages him, but the sacred sword appears ineffective. The wyrd retreats into the tower once again, and a new solution must be found.

4. **A Dying Clue**  
   One of the wyrd’s dying minions whispers the name *Mythrien Sarath*—an ancient elven god of Mythals and magic. The group must now find a way to call upon this forgotten deity to aid in the wyrd’s defeat and restore balance.

5. **The Buried Shrine**  
   Within the ruins, the party finds a journal describing a place where Mythrien’s name was once invoked in desperation—now swallowed by the earth. This is their next destination.

6. **Calling Mythrien**  
   Beneath Myth Unnohyr, the group discovers the sacred site. There, they call upon Mythrien Sarath. This time, the deity listens. His avatar manifests, promising to weaken the wyrd and grant the party safe passage into the tower.

7. **Final Confrontation**  
   The party enters the tower and battles the wyrd one final time. As the wyrd weakens, a cutscene begins. Mythrien appears and delivers the divine blow that breaks the wyrd's defenses. The group then strikes the final blow.

8. **Calming the Mythal**  
   With the wyrd defeated, Mythrien’s avatar uses his divine power to stabilize the corrupted mythal. The region will remain calm—at least for a time. This provides a window for the followers of Eldath and Mielikki to restore lasting peace.

9. **Resolution and Aftermath**  
   Back in Duskwood Dell, CHARNAME informs the High Priest and High Ranger of what transpired. The sacred sword remains with the party, useful for future threats such as Bodhi. Both churches vow to consider how Myth Unnohyr can be preserved—and whether it can ever truly be redeemed.
